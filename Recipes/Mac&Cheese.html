Mac & Cheese

Description

Mac & cheese is a down-home macaroni and cheese simple recipe. The recipe take 30 min around to prep all the ingredients and glassware to use.

It takes around 45 min to be cooked and good to eat.

Ingredients

  • 1 ¾ pounds whole-wheat macaroni
  • cup butter
  • ¾ cup all-purpose flour
  • 6 cups milk, divided
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
